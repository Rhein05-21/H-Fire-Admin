PROCESS BREAKDOWN:

1. HARDWARE LAYER:
   - Sensors detect smoke (PPM) or actual flame.
   - ESP32 publishes data every few seconds to the MQTT broker.

2. RESIDENT SIDE:
   - Receives instant updates. If a hazard is detected, the phone siren goes off.
   - Homeowner has the option to "Confirm" the fire or "Dismiss" (if it's a false alarm).

3. DATA BRIDGE:
   - Central hub that connects IoT data to the permanent database (Supabase).

4. ADMIN SIDE:
   - Monitoring center that sees all active alerts in the community.
   - Map automatically centers on the affected household using stored GPS data.

5. NOTIFICATIONS:
   - Critical alerts are sent via Push Notifications to ensure users see them immediately.

6. FUTURE AUTO-CALL LOGIC (PLANNED):
   - WHEN FIRE IS DETECTED: A countdown timer starts.
   - IF RESIDENT OR HOA DOES NOT CONFIRM/RESOLVE: The system will conclude 
     that the residents are unable to respond (possibly trapped or unconscious).
   - ACTION: The system will automatically trigger a voice call or digital 
     alert to the BFP (Bureau of Fire Protection) hotline with the exact 
     address and household details.
